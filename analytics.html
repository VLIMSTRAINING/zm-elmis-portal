Requisition & Reporting, AMC Colour Alerts

eLMIS Central Edition highlights products where the quantity requested is over the usual Average Monthly Consumption (AMC): Green normal, Red above AMC threshold, action needed.

Zambia's logistics system standardizes inventory using Months of Stock (MOS), with targets typically set at 3.0 MOS (up to 4.0 MOS for Essential Medicines at health centres). Two alerts trigger action: falling below 0.5 MOS (Emergency Order Point) or exceeding the maximum by over a month.
