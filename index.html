About eLMIS

A national, open-source logistics platform

eLMIS is built on OpenLMIS — open source, highly configurable, and API-enabled — running across two editions: Facility Edition, live in 1,710 health facilities, and Central Edition, providing nationwide oversight. Together they've cut report generation time from 15+ days to under a day.
