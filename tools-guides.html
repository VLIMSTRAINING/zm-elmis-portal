CHW / Satellite Site App

Download & install the mobile app

The Community Health Worker (CHW) / Satellite Site mobile app lets last-mile health workers record stock counts and consumption, even without internet access — data syncs automatically once back online.

eLMIS CHW App — v3.1
Android · 38 MB · Released 1 Apr 2026
⬇ Download APK

Installation Steps

  1. Enable installs from unknown sources. On your Android device go to Settings → Security, and allow installs from your browser or file manager.
  2. Download the APK using the button on this page over Wi-Fi where possible (file is ~38 MB).
  3. Open the downloaded file from your Downloads folder or notification tray and tap Install.
  4. Log in using the facility code and password issued by your District Pharmacist or eLMIS focal point.
  5. Sync your facility list the first time you're online — after that, the app works fully offline until you next have signal.
